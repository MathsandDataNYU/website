Welcome to the Mad site!

Wed, Sep 11, 2019

The Mad (Math and Data) group was created in fall 2016 by Afonso Bandeira, Joan Bruna and Carlos Fernandez-Granda, three Assistant Professors at the Courant Institute and the Center for Data Science.

We are always in the lookout for highly motivated students interested in understanding and developing the fundamental mathematical structures underpinning modern data analysis, machine learning, deep learning, and their applications.
